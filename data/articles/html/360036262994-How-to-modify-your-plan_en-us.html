Overview

This is a guide for users to modify your Linux and macOS plans. To change or cancel your plan, visit your org settings page (https://app.circleci.com/settings/plan/github/ORGNAME/overview) then follow the appropriate steps below. If you encounter any issues, contact billing@circleci.com. 

Note: Admin access is required to modify your plan. For more information, visit Am I an Org Admin?

Performance Plan 

Upgrade to the Performance Plan

Please visit your Plan Overview page of your account settings to view a cost estimate of the Performance Plan based on your team's typical monthly usage and team size.

As long as you are only making changes to your billing/payment information and continue to use the same account on CircleCI, your build history and project settings (env variables, advance settings, checkout keys, etc.) will remain intact.

Please be aware that changing your GitHub org and/or usernames can result in unintended side effects, which can cause your project settings to not sync over properly.

Make changes to your Performance Plan

If you find you need higher concurrency, contact billing@circleci.com to put in a request to increase concurrent jobs. The default limit is set to 40x parallelism for jobs to prevent credit overuse.

Cancel your Performance Plan

Go into your `Plan Overview` directly within your account and click `view and Configure Plan` button, then it redirects to the `billingportal` page. An admin can cancel the plan by clicking "Cancel" bottom on that page.

Note: Performance Plans will stay active until the end of your current billing cycle. 

Container Linux and macOS Plans

Note: Container plans are grandfathered cannot be re-purchased after cancellation.

Modify your Container Plan

On the plans page on the right-hand side, click on the 3 dots then "Edit"

Cancel your Container Plan

On the plans page on the right-hand side, click on the 3 dots then "Cancel"
