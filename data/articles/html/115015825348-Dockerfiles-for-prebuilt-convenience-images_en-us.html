CircleCI offers pre-built Docker Convenience Images found in our developer hub.

If you're interested to see the Dockerfiles for our pre-built convenience images located on DockerHub, you can find them here in this GitHub repository.

We have also introduced our next-generation convenience images, which have been built from the ground up with CI, efficiency, and determinism in mind. Some highlights of next-gen convenience images include faster spin-up times and greater reliability and scalability.
