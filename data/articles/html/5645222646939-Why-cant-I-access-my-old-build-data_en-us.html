Cannot find my old pipeline, workflow or job

If you cannot find an old pipeline, workflow or job on the UI or if you try and access them via the API, you see a not found error or it is not listed in the response, this means that the old build data falls outside of our data retention window.

Data retention policy updates

CircleCI has implemented a data retention policy for build data. Build data includes information on all pipelines run, workflows within those pipelines (including reruns), and jobs within those workflows which can be seen in the CircleCI UI or retrieved via the API.

This policy ensures we keep our data to a manageable size and our build systems performant and reliable as we grow.

Any other data, like workspaces, caches, or artifacts are governed by their existing policies.

As of June 30th, 2022, we retain build data, depending on the plan, for the following periods :

  • Free Plans: 90 days
  • Performance and Custom Plans: 180 days
  • Scale Plans: 2 years
