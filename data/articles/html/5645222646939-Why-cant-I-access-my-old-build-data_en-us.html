Retention policy updates

CircleCI has implemented a data retention policy. This policy ensures we keep our data to a manageable size as we grow. This only applies to build data (pipelines, workflows, and jobs). Any other data, like workspaces, caches, or artifacts are governed by their existing policies.

While our current limits are not finalized, we are currently enforcing the following limits:

  • Free Plans: 90 days
  • Performance and Custom Plans: 180 days
  • Scale Plans: 2 years.
