You may want to enable write-permissions or interact with other private repositories. 

To do so, you will need to add an SSH key to your CircleCI Pipeline with write-permissions. 

Check out our docs with complete instructions to generate and add the keys.

See GitHub and Bitbucket documentation for guidelines on storing SSH public keys:

  • https://developer.github.com/v3/guides/managing-deploy-keys
  • https://confluence.atlassian.com/bitbucket/use-deployment-keys-294486051.html
