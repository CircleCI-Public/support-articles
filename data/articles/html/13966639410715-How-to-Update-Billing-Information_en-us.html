How do I update my credit card?

Performance Plan 

  1. From your CircleCI UI landing page, click into the Billing tab. 
  2. Scroll down to the Payment Details section, click Edit. 
  3. From here, you can self-serve and update credit card information and billing address information.  
  4. Select Confirm Changes once complete. 
  5. Once this is complete, all future (billing statements will be sent) and charged based on this new information.

Where are my billing statements?

 

From the CircleCI Dashboard, navigate to the Billing tab. Once selected, billing statements will appear. If a billing statement is not available there, please reach out to Customer Success at  billing@circleci.com 

 

How do I cancel my subscription?

 

In your UI, navigate to the Billing tab. At the bottom of the page select “Looking to cancel your plan?” . Then follow the prompts to complete your cancellation.
