Overview

Users can sometimes wish to update their email used for CircleCI login. This can be accomplished in the user settings via the CircleCI UI.

 

Prerequisites

The user account must be one that signed up using their email on this page. Accounts created by signing in with your VCS (Bitbucket, GitHub, GitLab ) are not able to complete this action.

 

How to update your email in UI

To change your user email, please follow the steps below:

  1. Navigate to the user settings page

  2. Select Email from the left side bar

  3. Click Change Email which will load a form to fill out
