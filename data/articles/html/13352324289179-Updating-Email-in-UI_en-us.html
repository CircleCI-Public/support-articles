Overview

If you wish to update your email used for CircleCI login, this can be accomplished in your user settings via the CircleCI UI.

 

Prerequisites

You must be logged In with an account that signed up using email and password.. Accounts created by signing in with your VCS (Bitbucket, GitHub, GitLab ) are not able to complete this action.

 

How to update your email in UI

To change your user email, please follow the steps below:

  1. Navigate to the user settings page

  2. Select Email from the left side bar

  3. Click Change Email which will load a form to fill out

  4. Fill out this form with the desired new email to be used for the user in question and log in with the newly changed email
