On the notifications page, it is possible to set the notification settings per organization.

An example screenshot can be found below:

If you cannot find your organization in the list, you may need to follow at least one project for the organization - this is not related to the permission settings of the project within your VCS, but the matter of CircleCI permission.

Please make sure you are following projects here: https://app.circleci.com/projects/project-dashboard/<vcs>/<org-name>/

Following a project enables a user to subscribe to email notifications for the project build status and adds the project to their CircleCI dashboard.

Reference: https://circleci.com/docs/2.0/projects/#add-projects-page
