Overview 

If you signed up for CircleCI prior to September 2023 or are using an OAuth integration that has not been linked to your standalone email + password, you will need to authenticate your account/sign in via your VCS (GitHub or BitBucket)

How to sign into CircleCI

In order to sign into CircleCI using your VCS you can navigate to the following URL and click log in : https://circleci.com/vcs-authorize/

 

You can then click the drop down for "Signed up with GitHub or Bitbucket before
September 2023?" and select your VCS
