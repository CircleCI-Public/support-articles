Check Your Plan Information

The first thing to check when encountered with this error is if your current plan allows for the use of 2xlarge resource classes in Selecting Resource Classes. If not, you will need to upgrade to a plan that includes this resource class. If your plan does include this resource class, contact your Customer Success Manager or file a ticket with CircleCI Support to correct this error.
