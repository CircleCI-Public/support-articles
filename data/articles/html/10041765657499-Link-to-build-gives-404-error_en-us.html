Overview: 

When clicking a build link to a build that has exceeded the length of time per our data retention policy, you will receive a 404 error. Our data retention policy is dictated per your organization's plan. 

 

Depending on your plan, we retain build data for the following periods: 

  • Free Plans: 90 days
  • Performance and Custom Plans: 180 days
  • Scale Plans: 2 years
