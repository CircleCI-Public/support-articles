A course on CircleCI's feature, Config Policy

 

In this course we will cover CircleCI's feature, Config Policy. The "Introduction" and "Config Policies on CircleCI" sections are meant for those who want to get familiar with the feature and the ability to read a basic policy in Rego. The "Rego and Writing Policies" section is meant for those who want to get more hands on with Rego and begin writing their own policies.
