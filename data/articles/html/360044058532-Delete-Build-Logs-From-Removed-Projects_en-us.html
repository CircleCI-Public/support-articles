One Year Log Retention

Similar to Audit Logs, CircleCI retains build logs for up to one year based on the repository name. As a result, logging back in and re-setting up a project will retain the logs. In order to keep logs for both projects separate, we recommend you increment your repository name, i.e. project to project-1 or in a way that best suits your needs.

If you would like us to fully delete build logs associated with the project, please have an organization admin contact us at support@circleci.com or submit a ticket to fulfill this request.

If you would prefer to delete projects on your own rather than reaching out to Support, you can vote and comment and vote on an open feature request to support project deletion in the UI.

Note: Full project deletion is permanent and logs are unrecoverable once your request is complete.
