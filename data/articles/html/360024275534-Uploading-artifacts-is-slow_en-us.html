If you are uploading several hundred files as Artifacts and you are seeing long durations for the store_artifacts step(s), you should consider:

  1. Compressing all of the files.
  2. Uploading as a single compressed file.
- run:
    name: Compress Artifacts
    command: tar -cvzf myartifact.tar path/to/artifacts

- store_artifacts: 
    path: myartifact.tar
    destination: artifact_name
