CircleCI has support for python Django.

  • We have a blog post that will help you get set up with Django on CircleCI linked here: https://circleci.com/blog/continuous-integration-for-django-projects/

  • We also have a public repo on CircleCI-Public called circleci-demo-python-django which can be found here: https://github.com/CircleCI-Public/circleci-demo-python-django

If you have any issues with your Django setup always feel free to speak with our support team.
