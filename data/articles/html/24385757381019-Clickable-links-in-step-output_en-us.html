Overview

You can toggle "Accessible step output" off in order to make links in the CircleCI step output clickable. 

Toggling the "Accessible step output" feature: 

You can toggle this feature on/off from within the UI. To do this, please navigate to the jobs page: 

Toggling "Accessible step output" to "on" will disable clickable links. Links will be clickable if the feature is toggled "off" (see image above)
