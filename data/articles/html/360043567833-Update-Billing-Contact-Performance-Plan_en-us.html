Overview

Billing contacts will receive notifications regarding an accounts payment status. They will also receive payment receipts and invoices. 

Prerequisites 

These changes can only be made by an organization's admin. To help identify your organization's admins, please reference the articles below:

  • Am I an Org Admin?
  • How do I find my org admins?

 

Updating Billing Contact Email

  1. From your CircleCI UI landing page, click into the Billing tab.
  2. Scroll down to Billing Email section, select Edit.
  3. From here, you can self serve and update your billing email.
