Overview

Users may have noticed that recently, when opening another CircleCI session in another tab or window, you are logged out of the original tab or window.

 

Why is This Happening?

This is considered expected behavior. We implemented this change to increase the security for our users and minimize any risk involved with having multiple sessions open at once.
