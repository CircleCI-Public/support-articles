Overview

You may encounter times where our images are not up-to-date or possibly need a dependency that is not present. Our images and orbs are handled by our Community and Partner Engineering team.

 

Where can I submit requests for updates?

You can submit these to our CircleCI Public page:

https://github.com/CircleCI-Public

 

There under our repositories, you can find the image required and submit an issue:

https://github.com/orgs/CircleCI-Public/repositories
