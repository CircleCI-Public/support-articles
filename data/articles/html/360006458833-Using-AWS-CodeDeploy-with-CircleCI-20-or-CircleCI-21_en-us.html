Deploying to AWS CodeDeploy

If you're deploying your application to AWS CodeDeploy on CircleCI 2.0 or CircleCI 2.1, we recommend using the CircleCI AWS CodeDeploy orb: https://circleci.com/orbs/registry/orb/circleci/aws-code-deploy

Alternatively, you can use this helpful script: https://github.com/techpivot/aws-code-deploy
