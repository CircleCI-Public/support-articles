CircleCI currently provides support for customer issues in English and Japanese languages.

If you notice any issues or you have any questions, please feel free to get in touch with our support team by submitting a ticket in either of these languages. Our support engineers will get back to you as soon as possible and within the SLAs of your support plan.

You can also find further resources in both supported languages on the following links:

  • Community forum
    Where you can ask and answer questions, and discuss different topics with our community
  • Documentation (English, Japanese)
    Where you can find tutorials, samples and reference documentation
  • Support Center (English, Japanese)
    Where you can find additional support articles about frequently asked questions
