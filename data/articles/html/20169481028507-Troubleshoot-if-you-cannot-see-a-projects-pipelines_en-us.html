Overview

You may encounter an issue where you are not able to see one of your project's or pipelines.

Am I Following The Project?

Before any other action, visit your project dashboard to verify if you are currently following the project in question:

https://app.circleci.com/projects/project-dashboard/github/<your-username>/

I Am Following The Project

If you are following the project, but are unable to navigate to it our it's pipelines, we recommend going to your User Account Settings and selecting the "Refresh Permissions" options under the Account Integrations tab.

 

I Am NOT Following The Project

In this case, you will need to follow the project to see it's pipelines. Try following the project and ensure that it appears in your project dashboard view.
