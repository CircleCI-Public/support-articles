Overview

Sometimes you may be a part of an organization temporarily, or briefly working on an Open-Source Project that you no longer have any work for. In cases like this, you may want to remove your access to the organization in CircleCI to reduce on-screen clutter.

How Do I Remove The Org?

CircleCI inherits permissions and access from your VCS service. This means that if you would like to be removed from an organization in CircleCI, you will need to have an admin or owner in the org's VCS remove you in the organization settings in the VCS itself.

If you are an admin of the org, you can remove yourself, or if you would just like the organization removed from the organization dropdown in CircleCI, you can follow the steps found in this other article on our Support site:

  • Removing Unwanted Organizations from Drop-down Menu
