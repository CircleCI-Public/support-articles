How do I cancel my subscription?

In your UI, navigate to the Billing tab. At the bottom of the page select “Looking to cancel your plan?” .

 

 

From the drop down, select "Cancel Plan". 

 

 

A banner will appear letting you know your account is scheduled to cancel. The full cancellation will take place before your subscription renewal date. 

 

(Please note, any unused credits will be forfeited and not eligible for refund.)
