How do I cancel my subscription?

In your UI, navigate to the Billing tab. At the bottom of the page select “Looking to cancel your plan?” .

 

 

From the drop down, select "Cancel Plan". 

 

(Please note, any unused credits will be forfeited and not eligible for refund.)

 

Once Cancel Plan is selected, please follow the remaining prompts. The account will remain active till the end of the billing cycle. That date will be provided on the cancellation screen.

 

 

We recommend that you download all invoices before your account fully cancels. Once the account cancels, the Billing Tab will no longer be available. 

 

Customers who cancel their Performance Plan are downgraded to the Free plan, they can continue building on CircleCI but will be limited to 30,000 bonus credits per month and will lose access to premium features.


If you wish to upgrade back to the Performance plan and make use of the premium features, simply visit the Plan Overview page and complete the checkout process.
