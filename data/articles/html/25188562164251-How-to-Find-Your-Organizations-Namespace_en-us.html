Locate Namespace

If your org has published any orbs, navigate to your Organization Settings then the Orbs tab. From there, you can see your org namespace at the beginning of the orb name.

For example:

<namespace>/rails

If there aren’t any published orbs for your organization, you may still need to set up the namespace. Instructions on how to do that can be found here:

  • https://circleci.com/docs/2.0/orb-author-intro/#register-a-namespace
