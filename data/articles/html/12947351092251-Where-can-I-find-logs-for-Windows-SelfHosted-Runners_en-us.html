Overview

There may be times that your Self-Hosted Runners need troubleshooting. Obtaining and referencing the logs provided by your Runner may indicate problems you have with your builds.

 

For your investigation

On Windows, you can find your logs in the following:

C:\ProgramFiles\CircleCI\circleci-runner.log
